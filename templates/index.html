Katugunda Foundation

Katugunda Foundation stands as a beacon of hope for the underprivileged members of Kazo district, Uganda. These include children, orphans, women, disabled people, the poor, among others.

Through collaboration with individuals, churches, and businesses, we address the pressing needs of the most vulnerable.

We also engage in community development initiatives by creating a supportive environment where individuals can thrive and communities can flourish.

Join us in our mission to uplift lives and create a brighter tomorrow.
